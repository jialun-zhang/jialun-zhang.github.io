About Me

Hello! My name is Gavin. I'm currently a research scientist at Meta in the AI and Systems Co-Design team. I graduated in 2024 from the ECE department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My advisor was Richard Y. Zhang. I was also a member of the coordinated science laboratory at UIUC.

My work focuses on developing scalable training algorithms for optimization problems that arise in different area of machine learning, especially large language models. I'm interested in how adaptive and preconditioned first-order methods can effecitvely train large neural networks. I have published various first-author papers at top conferences like NeurIPS and top journals like JMLR.

Previously, I received a bachelor's degree in mathematics from Harvey Mudd College, and a master's degree in computational mathematics from Stanford.
